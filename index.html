Frequently Asked Questions

How fast will a professional arrive?

In most areas, our verified professionals reach your doorstep within 2 hours of booking. For scheduled bookings, you can pick any convenient time slot.

Is there a warranty on the service?

Yes. Every service comes with a 10-day warranty. If the same issue reoccurs within 10 days, we fix it again completely free of cost.

When do I need to pay?

You only pay after the service is completed to your satisfaction. Pricing is flat and transparent — no hidden charges.

Are the professionals background verified?

Yes, every professional on Domvex goes through identity verification and background checks before being onboarded onto the platform.

Can I cancel or reschedule my booking?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your booking from your dashboard before the professional is assigned, at no extra cost.
